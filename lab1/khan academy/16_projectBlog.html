BTS's Jungkook continues to write a new chapter in K-pop history.

The prestigious European music organization, the World Music Awards (WMA), announced that Jungkook's solo single "3D" has sold over 200,000 copies in the United Kingdom. This achievement earned him a Silver certification in the singles category from the British Phonographic Industry (BPI). With this, Jungkook set a record among K-pop solo artists, becoming the holder of five Silver certifications in the UK (for the songs "Standing Next to You," "Seven," "3D," "Left and Right," as well as the album GOLDEN).
